# Privacy Policy

**GIMMIE — X Bookmark Exporter**

GIMMIE is built to keep your data yours. In plain terms:

- **Nothing is collected.** GIMMIE has no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts.
- **Nothing leaves your computer.** Your bookmarks are read and turned into files entirely inside your own browser. No data is ever sent to us or to any third-party server, because there is no server.
- **Your export files stay local.** When you download a Markdown, PDF, JSON, CSV, or ZIP file, it goes to your device only. What you do with it after that is up to you.
- **NotebookLM is optional and separate.** If you choose "Send to NotebookLM," GIMMIE downloads your file locally and opens Google NotebookLM in a new tab. You upload the file yourself. GIMMIE does not transmit anything on your behalf. Anything you then upload to NotebookLM is governed by Google's privacy policy, not this one.

## What GIMMIE accesses, and why

- **Your X bookmarks page**, using your existing logged-in session, so it can read the posts you saved. This access is used only while you actively run a collection, and only to build the file you asked for.
- **Local browser storage**, to remember small preferences: your light/dark theme choice and the newest bookmark from your last scan (so "only new since last scan" can work). This never leaves your browser.

## What GIMMIE does not do

- It does not read other tabs or websites.
- It does not post, like, delete, or change anything on your X account.
- It does not store your bookmarks anywhere after the export is built.

## Permissions

GIMMIE requests only what it needs: access to X pages (to read your bookmarks), scripting (to run the collection on that page), and storage (to remember your preferences). It does not request access to your full browsing history.
